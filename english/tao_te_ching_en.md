# Tao Te Ching (English open translation)

This English translation is created for the Open Tao project and released under CC0 1.0 Universal.

## Chapter 1

The Tao that can be spoken is not the eternal Tao.
The name that can be named is not the eternal name.

The nameless is the beginning of heaven and earth.
The named is the mother of the ten thousand things.

Therefore, always be without desires to see its mystery.
Always have desires to see its manifestations.

These two arise together but differ in name;
Together they may be called the profound.
The profound of the profound,
The gateway to all wonders.
